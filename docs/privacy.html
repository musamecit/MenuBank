Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

MenuBank ("we", "our", or "us") is developed and operated by Musa Erdem Mecit (mecitmusa@gmail.com). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the MenuBank mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information: When you sign in with Google or Apple, we receive your name and email address. We store this information in our database (Supabase) to create and manage your account.

Location Data: With your permission, we access your device's location to show nearby restaurants. We do not store or share your location data.

User-Generated Content: If you upload restaurant menus or other content, this content is stored on our servers and may be visible to other users.

Restaurant Data: We display restaurant information sourced from Google Places API.

Usage Data: We collect anonymized data about how you interact with the app to improve the app experience.

Advertising

MenuBank displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

In-App Purchases

Restaurant owners may purchase a "Verified Badge" subscription ($9.99/month) through Apple's in-app purchase system.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

Data Retention

You may delete your account at any time.

Children's Privacy

MenuBank is not directed at children under 13.

Security

All data transmissions are encrypted using HTTPS.
