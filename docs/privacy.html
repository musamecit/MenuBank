Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

MenuBank (“MenuBank”, “we”, “our”, or “us”) is developed and operated by Musa Erdem Mecit. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, when we share it, how long we keep it, and what choices you have when using the MenuBank mobile application.

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the MenuBank mobile application and related services.

2. Information We Collect

a) Account Information

When you sign in using Google Sign-In or Sign in with Apple, we may receive information such as your name, email address, and the unique identifier provided by the sign-in provider. We use this information to create and manage your account, authenticate you, and provide app functionality.

b) User-Submitted Content

If you upload restaurant menus, images, comments, or other content, we collect and store that content so it can be displayed and used within the app.

c) Location Information

With your permission, the app may access your device location to help show nearby restaurants and location‑relevant content. Location access is used to support core app functionality. We do not sell location data, and we do not use precise location information to build behavioral profiles or for our own direct marketing. Some third‑party services used by the app (for example, mapping or places services) may process location‑related data as part of providing their functionality.

d) Usage and Diagnostic Information

We may collect limited usage, device, diagnostic, and performance information to maintain app security, fix bugs, improve reliability, and better understand app performance. Where possible, this information is aggregated or otherwise limited so that it is not used to personally identify you.

e) Subscription Information

If a restaurant owner purchases a subscription such as the Verified Badge plan, payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not receive or store your full payment card details. We may receive subscription status, transaction‑related confirmations, and entitlement information from Apple so we can manage access to subscription features.

f) Advertising Data

The app may display ads provided by Google AdMob. Google and its partners may process certain device or advertising‑related data in accordance with their own policies and configurations. For more information, you can review Google’s privacy information at https://policies.google.com/privacy.

3. How We Use Information

We may use collected information to:

  • create, authenticate, and manage user accounts;
  • provide core app features and show restaurant and menu content;
  • support uploads and community content sharing;
  • process and validate subscriptions and premium features;
  • maintain security and prevent abuse;
  • troubleshoot bugs and improve app reliability;
  • measure service performance and improve the user experience;
  • display advertising within the app.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information only as reasonably necessary with service providers and platform partners that help operate the app, including:

  • Supabase – for database, backend, and authentication infrastructure.
  • Google – for services such as Google Sign‑In, Google Places, and Google AdMob.
  • Apple – for Sign in with Apple, App Store infrastructure, and in‑app purchases.

Where third‑party services process data on our behalf or in connection with app functionality, they handle data under their own terms, privacy notices, and applicable legal requirements.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this Privacy Policy, including account administration, security, legal compliance, dispute resolution, and enforcement of our terms.

If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data and associated account records within a reasonable period, except where retention is required by law, required for legitimate security or fraud‑prevention purposes, or necessary to complete obligations related to active disputes or transactions.

6. Account Deletion and User Choices

If you created an account, you can request or initiate account deletion from within the app (for example, from the profile or settings area). Once your deletion request is processed, your account and associated personal data will be removed or anonymized, subject to the limited retention described above.

You can also control certain permissions, such as location access, from your device settings.

7. Children’s Privacy

MenuBank is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 13, we will take appropriate steps to delete it.

8. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

9. International Processing

Depending on the infrastructure and service providers used to operate the app, information may be processed in countries other than your country of residence. Where required, appropriate safeguards are used to help protect your information.

10. Third‑Party Services

The app may rely on third‑party services and platforms, including Apple, Google, Supabase, and related infrastructure providers. Those third parties may process data under their own privacy notices and terms. We encourage you to review those policies where relevant.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after an update means the revised Privacy Policy will apply going forward.
